My name is Stasy Tsai-Hsuan HSIEH.

Tainan, Taiwan witnessed my birth in 1993. Having been inspired both by Oscar Wilde and Sherlock Holmes at 5 year-old, I was always exploring different domains of knowledge---be it school education, or self-taught ones.

Later on I realized that I cannot be what I do not see, therefore lived in various countries.

With physics as an international language, I have performed a wide range of careers-- from fundamental physics teacher, astrophysicist researcher, scientific interpreter, to semiconductor research engineer. Somehow in my industrial path I found myself an enabler of the technology that aggregates human isolation.

“ With the hope of offering next-generation a broader vision of life choices, I started a local community that brought international scientists, engineers and artists in Taiwan together to tell high school students what we are doing, and how we might be able to collaborate.”

In my leisure time I swim, do yoga, dance, read, write and design.
