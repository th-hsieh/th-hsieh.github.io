On Healthcare, Gender Equality, Educational System
PUBLIC
Publicity and privacy seemed opposite but a two-sided fact: what we do are supposedly private but no longer---what we see as private choices form a trend, the trend of our generation becomes a common, the common then becomes public. Grass isn't always greener on the other side. Each system has its own reason, which might not be relevant anymore. We can take care of the land where we are HERE AND NOW. Starting from revisiting the incidents in a general scale, I will propose feasible solutions in a bottom-up manner.

PRIVACY

I believe my private travails happened to many others as well. Inspired by my life in 7 countries, I am creating a safe communicative hub for the excursious to voice out. When the voices form resonant echos strongly enough, changes in organizations occur, and evolution begins. Let me bare honest witness to the world as I have experienced with it. While living abroad sounds exciting, it is in reality bitter realizations. Health care, gender equality and educational system are the issues I struggled with the most. It might not be pleasant to look at the facts, but I do believe it is a great opportunity to examine the necessary changes.
