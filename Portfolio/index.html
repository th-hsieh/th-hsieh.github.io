    Publicity ≠ Privacy ?

    What we experience are supposedly private but no longer. What we live through privately forms a trend, the trend of our generation becomes common,widely discussed, and public.

    Healthcare, Educational System, Gender Equality!

    I believe my private travails are not just mine, but many others' as well. Inspired greatly by the general Taiwanese education, the German and Swiss health care, and the gender inequality in techno-realm, I am keen to create a safe communicative hub for the excursions to voice their experiences. When the voices form resonant echos strongly enough, changes in organizations will occur accordingly, and evolution begins.

    About MATRIX MASTER PRO

    Matrix Master Pro is a free Matrix code rain/digital rain animation generator created with HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript. It is designed as a independent research and art project with the purpose to explore the possibilities of modern web technologies.
